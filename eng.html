The workflow

On-site to paid in four steps.

1
Walk the job

Pull up the app and enter your measurements as you walk the site. The deck and fence estimators run the math for you.

2
Build the list

Measurements automatically populate a supply list. Add anything it missed, adjust quantities, done.

3
Write the quote

Line up materials and labor, set your tax rate, and the total is ready. Print it or save it as a PDF right there.

4
Save the file

Download the project file to your phone or laptop. Open it next week when the client calls with questions.

Privacy

Nothing goes anywhere you didn't put it.

Client names, addresses, and prices never touch a server. The whole tool runs in your browser. When you hit "Download," the file goes straight to your computer — period.

  • No account required
  • No monthly fee
  • No internet connection needed after the page loads
  • Client data stays on your device
